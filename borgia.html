    POPE BORGIA’S PARTY


    Anna Oramo and Teppo Lampela, voice
    Renaissance ensemble (3 musicians)

    The Pope Rodrigo Borgia (1431–1503) was known as an immoral pope. This program reflects the story of his life as a Spaniard, lover, dedicated father, generous mecenate and a man of the Church.

    Music by: Anonymous, Josquin Desprez, Leonora d’Este, Hans Kotter, Heinrich Isaac, Francesco Patavino, Bartolomeo Tromboncino
