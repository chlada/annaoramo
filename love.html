    HEAVENLY LOVE, EARTHLY LOVE
    Rakkaus taivaassa ja maan päällä

    Anna Oramo, voice
    Hanna Haapamäki, traverso
    Laura Ollberg, harpsichord

    This beautiful trio program reflects love in heavenly and earthly context. The fabulous cantatas and motets of the high baroque are represented among virtuosic instrumental music.

    Music by André Campra, Jacques Martin Hotteterre – Tarquinio Merula – Alessandro Scarlatti – Domenico Scarlatti – Antonio Vivaldi
