    Amor céu Medieval Ensemble
    Aino Peltomaa, voice, mediaeval harp, percussion
    Eira Karlson, fiddle
    Corina Marti, recorders, organetto
    Anna Oramo, voice, clavisimbalum

    Amor céu invites you to join the waves of colorful and emotional female-voiced love poetry. Their program is build around the Cantigas d’Amigo, a collection of lyric love poetry from the Iberian Peninsula, dating back to 1200-1300s. A distinguished feature of these cantigas is that the speaker is always a female; a mother, a daughter, a friend, a bride, a girl, a wife.

    Music from Cantigas d’Amigo, Codex Montpellier, Libre Vermell de Montserrat, Cantigas de Santa Maria
