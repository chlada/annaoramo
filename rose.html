    ROMAN DE LA ROSE

    Tuuli Lindeberg, voice
    Anna Oramo, voice, clavisimbalum, virginal

    The gestured duo program is an intimate triumph of storytelling with music. The program is inspired by the Medieval Roman de la Rose (13th Century), where is depicted a dream like journey inside a secret garden. A young man is wandering in the garden, looking for “Rose” that symbolises love and sexuality. He meets various aspects of goodness and evil on his way.

    Music by: Anonymous, Thoinot Arbeau, Pierre Attaignant, Gilbert de Berneville, Gilles Binchois, William Byrd, Antonio Cabezón, Jehan Chadravoine, Guillaume Dufay, Jean Lescurel, Folquet de Marseille, Guillaume de Machaut, Jaufre Rudel, Claudin de Sermisy, Philippe de Vitry
