    WOMAN AND THE MYSTICAL LOVE - FROM HILDEGARD TO MESSIAEN

    Anna Oramo, voice and clavisimbalum
    Maria Männikkö, piano

    Source: Wikipedia

    This duo concert for a pianist and a singer reflects the thematics of mystical love from the woman’s point of wiew, through centuries. A woman is here represented both as the creator (composer) and an object of love. The three magnificent women composers are presented together with their male adorers. The strong mediaeval vocal music of Hildegard vo Bingen and the Trobairitz Comtessa de Dia meet songs by Robert Schumann and Olivier Messiaen, and the persuasive and mystical piano music by Clara Schumann and Messiaen.

    Music by: Hildegard von Bingen, Comtessa de Dia, Olivier Messiaen, Clara Schumann, Robert Schumann
