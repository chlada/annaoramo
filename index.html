Anna Oramo
harpsichordist, vocalist

Music starts from silence

“… she not only offers large-scale contrast - she's also alert to the quicksilver wit in the details.”

(Finnish Music Quarterly)

Anna Oramo is best known as a harpsichord player, leader and soloist, having recorded and performed around the world with numerous baroque orchestras and ensembles, such as the Helsinki Baroque Orchestra, the Finnish baroque Orchestra, Orfeo 55, Ensemble Matheus...

Anna Oramo is a singer, focusing in Medieval, Renaissance and Baroque Music, and practising self accompaniment. In this field she performs with Amor céu medieaval ensemble and the Finnish Baroque orchestra.

Anna Oramo has recorded for labels Aeolus, Alba, Erato and Naxos. A recording of J.S. Bach harpsichord concertos with The Helsinki Baroque Orchestra won a Diapason d'Or in 2020.
